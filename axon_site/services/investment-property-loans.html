Financing Your Investment

Investment loans differ from owner‑occupied mortgages. They may have higher rates and different servicing requirements. We help you understand:

  • Loan types: Principal & interest vs interest‑only, line of credit facilities and split loans.
  • Deposit options: Using equity from existing properties or cash savings as your deposit.
  • Tax considerations: We work alongside your accountant to ensure you structure your loan in a tax‑effective manner.
  • Lender policies: Each lender has unique criteria around rental income and borrowing capacity for investment loans.

Our goal is to help you select a loan that supports your investment strategy while balancing risk and cash flow.
