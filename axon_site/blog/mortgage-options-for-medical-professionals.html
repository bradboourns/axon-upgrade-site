Mortgage Options for Medical Professionals

Published: 10 August 2025

Many lenders offer specialised products and policy exceptions for medical professionals. These can include lower deposits, interest rate discounts and higher borrowing limits.

If you're a doctor, nurse or allied health worker, consider speaking with a broker who understands lender policies for your profession.
